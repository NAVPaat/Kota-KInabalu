Although the peak of Mount Kinabalu is a dream for climbers, not everyone can reach the top. There are various factors that prevent climbers from reaching the top. Among them, the limit on the number of climbers allowed to climb in a day in addition to personal stamina factors and the cost of the package.
In terms of climbing, it can be divided into two stages:
1. From Gate Timpohon to Laban Rata
2. From Laban Rata to Low's Peak (the peak of Mount Kinabalu)

The construction work of a new route to the summit of Mount Kinabalu, which was recently underway, has led to the discovery of several endemic flora species including orchids. Sabah Parks Research and Education Division Assistant Director, Rimi Repin said, since all plant species on the highest peak in Asia are considered unique and special, the endemic species discovered need to be removed from the route currently being built. The endemic species were found on the new trail at KM6.5 of Mount Kinabalu and were removed using the translocation method to reduce the loss of flora and fauna species.
